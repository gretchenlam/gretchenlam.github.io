Welcome to Raccoon Saga!

a matching game

Raccoon Saga is a Candy Crush-like game that allows users to make matches of three and four! Simply click and drag a raccoon to a corresponding tile to create a match. Upon making a match, new tiles are randomly generated above and fall into place. The user's score is also logged on the side of the screen.

Click here to try it out!

This game was made using React and is hosted with Firebase.
